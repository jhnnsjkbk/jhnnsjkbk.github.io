## 🏆 Recognitions


**ESA Φ-lab: Invited Visiting Researcher (2026)** :satellite:

I have been selected as an invited visiting researcher at the ESA Φ-lab in April-May 2026 in Frascati, Italy to support our shared IBM-ESA research agenda.

**NASA Agency Group Award (2025)** ⭐

The development team around the deep learning models of the Prithvi and INDUS family received the NASA Agency Group Award in summer 2025:

> <small>*For developing AI foundational models that enhance scientific analysis, facilitating breakthrough discoveries, and exemplifying collaborative innovation, setting new standards for transparency and scientific rigor in AI development.*</small>

**IBM Outstanding Accomplishment Award (2025)**

My work on multimodal deep learning for Earth observation has been awarded with an IBM Outstanding Accomplishment Award.

**IBM Significant External Honor (2025)**

I was recognized with an IBM Significant External Honor for my contributions in geospatial AI as part of our collaboration with NASA.

**AGU Open Science Recognition Prize (2025)**

The Prithvi teams have received the 2025 AGU Open Science Recognition Prize.

**NASA MSFC Honor Award (2024)**

I received the NASA Honor Award from Marshall Space Flight Center (MSFC) for my contributions to deep learning for earth observations.

**IBM Outstanding Accomplishment Award (2024)**

My work on deep learning for weather and climate assessments have been awarded with an IBM Outstanding Accomplishment Award.

**Tenure-Track Professorship Recommendation (2024)**

I was fortunate enough to be recommended for a tenure-track professorship at a German university of excellence in winter 2024. I decided to stay in industry research.

**Dissertation Award ⭐ (2024)**

My dissertation *Data-Centric Artificial Intelligence: Foundations and Methods for Deep Learning* was recognized as one of the most outstanding theses in Germany by the Association for Electrical, Electronic & Information Technologies.

**Dissertation Award Nomination (2024)**

My thesis *Data-Centric Artificial Intelligence: Foundations and Methods for Deep Learning* has been nominated for a faculty-wide dissertation award by Karlsruhe Institute of Technology.

**IBM Outstanding Accomplishment Award (2023)**

In summer 2023, my contributions on pioneering deep learning to accelerate scientific discovery have been awarded with an IBM Outstanding Accomplishment Award.

**Outstanding Dissertation (2023)**

I defended my Ph.D. thesis with *summa cum laude*.

**Best Paper Award at HHAI (2023)**

Our paper on the interdependence of reliance behavior and accuracy in AI-assisted decision-making got awarded with the best paper award at HHAI'23! [Paper](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2304.08804.pdf) | [Ranking](https://hhai-conference.org/2023/accepted-papers/)

**Best Paper Award Nomination at WI (2023)**

Our paper on improving the efficiency of human-in-the-loop systems was nominated for the best paper award at WI'23 and selected for a thought-provoking session. [Nomination](https://wi2023.de/en/best-paper-nominations/)

**DAAD Scholarship for International Research on AI (2020)**

I received a DAAD scholarship as part of my master's thesis supporting research on AI.
